Our Story
The Journey of UG Bazaar
🏪
📍 Bhangaram, Talodhi

UG Bazaar started with a small dream — Uday Ainchwar ji along with his family laid the foundation of this shop. His one goal was to bring quality products to every home in Bhangaram and Talodhi, at honest and affordable prices.

What started as just a Kirana store has now expanded into 8+ departments. Hardware, Electrical, Electronics, Furniture, Krushi Kendra — everything is now available under one roof.

We believe that every customer is a member of our family. That's why we don't just sell products — we sell trust and relationships.

"At UG Bazaar, our mission is simple — bring the city to your doorstep, so you never have to leave your village for anything." — UG Bazaar
